How to Play

  • Flip cards by clicking on them to find matching pairs
  • You have a limited number of moves to find all pairs
  • The game has three difficulty levels:
    • Easy: 4×4 grid, 25 moves limit
    • Medium: 4×5 grid, 30 moves limit
    • Hard: 5×6 grid, 45 moves limit
  • Timer starts when you flip the first card
  • Match all pairs before running out of moves to win!
